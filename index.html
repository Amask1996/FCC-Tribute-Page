The Band American Football
Outline about the band american football

American Football is an Indie Rock/Emo band born out of Illinois in 1997. The band was formed by guitarist and lead singer Mike Kinsella (of Cap'n Jazz), drummer Steve Lamos and guitarist Steve Holmes. Between 1997 and 2000 released two albums American Football EP and their titular album American Football. Following a 14 year hiatus the band reunited in 2014 and released a deluxe edition of their self-titled album along with playing several shows and producing a music video for their arguably most popular song Never Meant.

American Football have become one of the most popular and influential bands in the genre of indie and emo rock, notably for their experimentation with alternative tunings, unusual time signatures, and high technical playing. American Football has used these elements to create a highly unique sound. The bands sound is atmospheric, often expressing their musics sadder messages of loss, heart break, and growing up.

As of 2019 the band has released 5 albums, with plans to continue touring (pending Covid restrictions).
